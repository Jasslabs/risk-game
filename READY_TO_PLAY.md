## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start Game** → Get assigned territories
2. **Pick Territory** → Click one you own (highlighted in gold)
3. **Attack** → Click adjacent enemy territory
4. **Battle** → Dice roll automatically, losses applied
5. **Conquer** → Keep attacking until you win!
6. **Win** → Be the first to own all 10 territories
